The Brown Bear

The brown bear is a large bear species found across Eurasia and North America. It is one of the most widely distributed bear species in the world.

Learn more about Brown Bears

Types of Brown Bears

  • Grizzly Bear
  • Kodiak Bear
  • Himalayan Brown Bear

Top Countries with Brown Bears

  1. Russia
  2. United States
  3. Canada

Diet

Brown bears are omnivores. They eat fish, berries, and plants.

Lifespan

Brown bears live up to 25 years in the wild.
